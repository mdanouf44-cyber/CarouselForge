# ⚡ LinkedIn Content Strategy & Niches

This document details the content strategy, target niches, and scheduling logic for **Mohammad Anouf Saani's** LinkedIn presence, powered by **Carousel Forge**.

---

## 🎯 Target Content Niches

The automated pipeline actively curates and generates content focusing on highly technical, actionable, and educational themes. The content is divided into four core areas:

### 1. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML)
* **LLMs & SLMs:** Updates on state-of-the-art models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta Llama, Google Gemini, and open-weight alternatives).
* **Local AI Execution:** Running LLMs locally on consumer hardware using tools like **Ollama**, **Llama.cpp**, and **vLLM**.
* **AI Research & Development:** High-signal scientific papers and breakthroughs curated directly from the **Hugging Face Daily Papers API**.

### 2. Agentic AI & Advanced Developer Workflows
* **Multi-Agent Frameworks:** Orchestration platforms like **CrewAI** and **AutoGen**.
* **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):** Context ingestion, vector databases, chunking strategies, and embeddings.
* **Integrations & Protocols:** Implementation of the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**, custom prompt engineering, and node-based workflows (e.g., **ComfyUI**).
* **Developer Guides:** Practical "how-to" tutorials on building production-grade AI pipelines.

### 3. Open-Source Software (OSS) & Developer Tooling
* **GitHub Trends:** Curation of rising open-source repositories and utility tools.
* **Developer Tooling:** CLI utilities, deployment setups (Docker, VPS), APIs, and backend architectures.
* **Automation Pipelines:** End-to-end automation strategies for engineering teams.

### 4. Tech Ecosystem & High-Signal Trends
* **Engineering Discussions:** Tech highlights, system design patterns, and engineering debates sourced from **Hacker News** and **OpenAI Developer Forums**.
* **Emerging Tech News:** Practical tech breakthroughs from vetted sources like TechCrunch AI, VentureBeat AI, and Wired.

---

## 🚫 Content Filters (What is Avoided)

To maintain a high-value, developer-first, and professional feed, Carousel Forge implements a strict keyword penalty algorithm ($0.2\times$ weight reduction) to filter out noise:

* 📉 **Corporate Gossip:** CEO changes, board disputes, and tech-industry layoffs.
* 💰 **Finance & Markets:** Tech stock performance, quarterly earnings, and acquisitions.
* ⚖️ **Legal & Regulatory:** Lawsuits, policy battles, antitrust cases, and regulatory crackdowns.
